Bridges to US Degrees

Our Mission

Bridges to US Degrees is a program dedicated to helping Ukrainian students apply to colleges in the United States. Our mission is to provide guidance, resources, and support to talented Ukrainian students who aspire to pursue higher education in America.

We aim to bridge the gap between Ukrainian students and US educational institutions by offering:

  • Application assistance
  • Standardized test preparation
  • Guidance on selecting suitable colleges
  • Information on scholarships and financial aid
  • Cultural preparation for studying in the US

    • Note: The application portal is currently under construction, applications submitted cannot be processed until further notice.

Through our program, we hope to create opportunities for Ukrainian students to access world-class education and foster international understanding and cooperation.
